ITsatAction
This application shows the satisfaction of IT end users over a specific period. The results can be displayed per country (map view) or by other attributes in the list view. The period can be changed by clicking on the date range in the upper left corner.
Filters
In the navigation panel on the left, data filters can be set. These filters are applied in the map or the list view. Filters can be set for geographic, organization and ticket related attributes.
Scores
The score ‘box’ in the navigation panel shows the scores for different service areas. You can select a score to be displayed on the map or list view. Double clicking a score gives the underlying questions for that service area.
